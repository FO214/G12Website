Reflection

Though when I initially taught myself computer science, sorting was one of the first topics I learned, I was very limited in the sorts I knew how to do. Doing this assignment not only taught me that there are far more sorts that exist, but I learned the concepts behind many of them as well and the theory of why exactly they work. Choosing three to code allowed me to learn how to implement three of these theoretics in a manner such that I don't think I'll forget it any time soon. I believe that this helped me grow as a programmer due to opening my options to more sorting methods, and learning how to implement theory topics much better.
